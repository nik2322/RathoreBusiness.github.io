About Us

Founded in 2020 in Delhi, Webeinfo was born to help school franchises win in the digital age. We understand the real struggle — managing multiple branch websites, enquiries, and brand consistency can quickly turn into chaos.

That’s why we built a smart CMS platform that helps you launch branch microsites in minutes, track leads from every source, and manage everything from one simple dashboard — no coding, no extra staff.

Our mission is simple: empower educational brands to grow faster, save time, and attract more students — effortlessly.
